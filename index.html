About Me

My name is Patrick Caserta and I was born and raised in Midlothian, VA. Growing up I devoloped a huge interest in Video games. I also played the drums, and was active in all kinds of sports. My favorite sport is basketball, because when I was younger I was much taller than the rest of the kids, giving me a huge advantage on the court. Later on in highschool and middle school I got into lacrosse. I would enventually play for my highschool, playing at both JV and varsity levels.
